Welcome to Four Fifths Jazz

Four Fifths Jazz Ensemble is formed of Piano/Vocals, Double Bass, Drums, Trumpet & Sax. The ensemble play their own compositions along side all the classics from Ella Fitzgerald to Duke Ellington.

The ensemble perform concerts all over the country and are also available for private functions (Parties / Weddings / Corporate Events).
