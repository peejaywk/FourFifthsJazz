About Us

Four Fifths Jazz are a group of accomplished musicians who are former students of the Royal Northern College of Music. Four Fifths Jazz are a quartet that consists of vocals/piano, double bass, trumpet/sax and drums. However, if a bigger sound is required the line up can be extended as required. The ensemble have played concerts all over the country and are available for private hire. They are the ideal choice for corporate events, dinner parties and weddings providing tailor-made sets to create the perfect atmosphere.
