About Us

Four Fifths Jazz is a group of accomplished musicians who share a passion for Jazz and Swing music. Four Fifths Jazz are a quartet that consists of vocals/piano, double bass, trumpet/sax and drums. However, if a bigger sound is required the line-up can be extended as required. The ensemble has played concerts all over the country and is available for private hire. They are the ideal choice for corporate events, dinner parties and weddings providing tailor-made sets to create the perfect atmosphere.

Influences

The ensemble is heavily influenced by the great American Jazz singers and groups from the 50s, 60s and 70s. These include Ella Fitzgerald, Louis Armstrong, Duke Ellington, Dinah Washington and the Ramsey Lewis Trio. Modern interpretations from these artists and The Great American Songbook form a major part of the repertoire along with new compositions.
